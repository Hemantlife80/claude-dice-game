Information We Collect

Our 3D Dice Game is designed to be a simple and fun experience. We do not collect any personally identifiable information from our users.

  • Player Names: The names you enter for players are stored only on your local device (in your browser) for the duration of your game session. This information is never sent to our servers and is deleted when you close the browser tab.
